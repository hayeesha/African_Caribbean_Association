ACA's PURPOSE

The ACA is an organization for African and Caribbean Faculty, Alumni, Students and Friends of the University of Mississippi. The purpose of the organization is to address issues affecting African and Caribbean students and to facilitate cultural diversity, awareness and social interaction at the University of Mississippi.
